Story Vocabulary

English Word Chinese Translation Example Explanation
Boisterous 喧闹的 / 活泼的
(xuānnào de / huópō de) 		The **boisterous** crowd celebrated the victory with loud cheers. Emphasizes noisy and lively energy. 喧闹的 is often used for loud activity.
Gravitas 庄严 / 庄重感
(zhuāngyán / zhuāngzhòng gǎn) 		The judge delivered the sentence with appropriate **gravitas** and seriousness. Refers to serious, dignified importance or weightiness.
Mired 陷入困境的 / 泥泞的
(xiànrù kùnjìng de / níníng de) 		The project became **mired** in bureaucratic red tape and delays. Literally means stuck in mud, but often used figuratively as stuck in a difficult situation (陷入困境的).
Pall 笼罩的阴影 / 阴郁气氛
(lǒngzhào de yīnyǐng / yīnyù qìfēn) 		A **pall** of silence fell over the town after the sudden storm. The feeling of gloom, sadness, or darkness covering a place or event.
Perfunctory 敷衍的 / 草率的
(fūyǎn de / cǎoshuài de) 		The tour guide gave a **perfunctory** explanation before rushing us to the next stop. Doing something carelessly or out of duty, without real effort or interest.
Eloquent 雄辩的 / 意味深长的
(xióngbiàn de / yìwèi shēncháng de) 		The painting was an **eloquent** statement on the tragedy of war. Can mean persuasive (雄辩的), but in the story, it means deeply expressive or meaningful (意味深长的).
Monumental 巨大的 / 意义重大的
(jùdà de / yìyì zhòngdà de) 		Rebuilding the ancient temple was a **monumental** task that took decades. Refers to something massive in size (巨大的) or extremely important (意义重大的).
Recast 重塑 / 重新表达
(chóngsù / chóngxīn biǎodá) 		She **recast** her business plan to focus on sustainability rather than just profit. To change the form, structure, or presentation of something.
